Tech Heroes

Thomas Bengry

Contributed by: Victoria Bengry

My father, Thomas Bengry, is my tech hero. As Director of Technology for Markesan District Schools, he works to ensure that low-income students have access to the technology they need to succeed. He often hosts coding events to teach children programming skills. His dedication to bridging the digital divide inspires me to do the same.

Kimberly Bryant

Contributed by: Malak Bokhari

Kimberly Bryant is the founder of Black Girls CODE, an organization dedicated to empowering young girls of color to embrace the tech industry. Her work to promote inclusivity and representation in STEM fields makes her a true hero in the tech space, showing that diversity drives innovation.
